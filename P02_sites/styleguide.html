Brand Definition

Jay.Kay Customs is a brand that offers sneaker customization for sneakerheads since May 2018. We are an exclusive footwear customization brand creating unique designs and adding vibrant enahncements to classic sneakers that is one of a kind. It first started as a hobby between Jisoo and Kirby, it then turned from passion into business venture where they could create designs for others that no one else had.
